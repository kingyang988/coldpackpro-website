FAQ
What is your production lead time?

Standard orders are usually 10-20 days depending on product type, quantity, and packaging. Custom projects may need extra time for confirmation and sampling.

Can you customize the products?

Yes. We support size, color, logo, material structure, cooling medium, weight, freezing point, and packaging customization.

What is the customization cycle and can I get samples?

Simple logo or packaging changes can be confirmed quickly. New structure or formula development usually takes 7-15 days for sampling and approval. Standard samples can usually be arranged in 3-7 days.

What files and formats do you need?

For artwork, AI, PDF, EPS, or SVG is preferred. For dielines or carton design, AI/PDF works best. A clear logo, size reference, and usage description are enough to start.

Do you support mold opening and packaging customization?

Yes, mold opening is available for projects that need a new shape or special structure. Inner bag, carton, label, master pack, and retail packaging can be customized for your brand and market requirements.

What certificates and MOQ do you offer?

Available documents may include MSDS, material declarations, food-contact related documents, test reports, and export files. MOQ depends on the model and customization level; contact us for the best quotation.
